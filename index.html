Privacy Policy for Garden Words

Effective date: 26 May 2026
App: Garden Words
Developer: likemelinkme
Contact: support@likemelinkme.com

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use Garden Words ("the App"), available on Google Play for Android devices. By downloading or using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

Summary

Garden Words is a relaxing word puzzle game. We do not run our own servers that collect your personal information, and your game progress (your garden, streaks, and in-game currency) is stored locally on your device. The App uses third-party services — Google AdMob (advertising), Unity (game engine and analytics), and Google Play (app distribution and subscriptions) — and these services collect certain data according to their own privacy policies.

Information we collect

Information you provide directly

The App does not require you to create an account, and we do not collect your name, email, or other directly identifying information through the App itself.

Information stored on your device

Your game data — including your garden, plants, streaks, in-game currency, settings, and whether you have an active Garden Pro subscription — is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Information collected automatically by third-party services

Google AdMob (advertising): The App displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process data such as your device's advertising identifier, IP address, device information, and app-usage data in order to serve and measure ads. In particular, Google AdMob may use a device advertising identifier to serve and measure ads. You can reset this identifier or opt out of ad personalization in your device settings under Settings → Google → Ads.Learn more: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Unity (game engine and analytics): The App is built with Unity and may use Unity's services, including analytics and in-app purchasing infrastructure. Unity may collect device and usage information to support app functionality, analytics, and crash reporting. Learn more: https://unity.com/legal/game-player-and-app-user-privacy-policy

Google Play (distribution and subscriptions): The App is distributed through Google Play, and the optional Garden Pro subscription is processed through Google Play's billing system. Google Play handles all payment processing; we never receive or store your payment-card details. Learn more: https://policies.google.com/privacy

How we use information

Information collected by these third-party services is used to display and measure advertising (AdMob), support core app functionality, analytics, and stability (Unity), process and manage the optional Garden Pro subscription (Google Play), and understand general, aggregated usage to improve the App. We do not use your information to identify you personally.

Advertising

The App is supported by ads, shown to users who do not subscribe to Garden Pro. Ads are served by Google AdMob. Depending on your settings and region, you may see personalized or non-personalized ads. You can control ad personalization through your device settings (for example, by resetting or deleting your advertising ID in your Android settings). Subscribers to Garden Pro do not see ads.

Subscriptions

Garden Pro is an optional auto-renewing monthly subscription processed through Google Play. Payment is charged to your Google Play account. Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your Google Play account settings. We do not process or store any payment information.

Children's privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us at support@likemelinkme.com and we will take appropriate steps.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information is processed by the third-party services described above (Google AdMob, Unity, Google Play) under their own privacy policies. We do not share your information with other third parties except as required by law.

Your choices and rights

  • Ad personalization: Manage through your device's advertising settings.
  • Local data: Uninstalling the App removes the game data stored on your device.
  • Subscriptions: Manage or cancel through Google Play.
  • Depending on your region (for example, the EEA/UK under GDPR, or California under CCPA/CPRA), you may have additional rights regarding data collected by the third-party services. Please refer to those services' privacy policies (linked above), or contact us at support@likemelinkme.com.

Data Deletion

Garden Words stores your game data — including your garden, progress, streaks, and settings — locally on your device. This data is not stored on our servers.

To delete this data, simply uninstall the App from your device; doing so removes all locally stored game data.

The App uses third-party services (Google AdMob, Unity, and Google Play) that may collect certain data as described in this Privacy Policy. Data held by those services is governed by their own privacy policies, and you can manage or request deletion of that data through their respective settings and tools (for example, by resetting or deleting your device's advertising ID in your Android settings).

If you have any questions or wish to make a data-related request, please contact us at support@likemelinkme.com. We will respond to legitimate requests within a reasonable timeframe.

Data retention

Game data is retained locally on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App. Data collected by third-party services is retained according to their respective policies.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@likemelinkme.com.
